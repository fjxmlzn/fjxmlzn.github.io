Summary

I am the co-founder and developer of Luogu website (http://www.luogu.org/) which is designed for students who learn computer algorithms. It provides a platform for students to automatically grade their codes, organize and take part in algorithm contests and exchange ideas and thoughts. By the end of Oct. 2016, there are more than 27,000 registered users and total page views exceed 10,000,000.
